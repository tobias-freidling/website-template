BETS: The dangers of selection bias in early analyses of the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic

Tobias Freidling, Phyllis Ju, Sergio Bacallado, Rajen Shah
April 2020
PDF Code Dataset Project Slides Video DOI

Abstract

We developed a generative model for key epidemiological events in COVID-19 patients and derived explicit formulae to correct for selection bias. We find that several early analyses of the COVID-19 pandemic were severely biased due to sample selection.
